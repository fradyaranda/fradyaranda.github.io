Chronic Lower Back Pain Treatment

Sora Blogging Tips 8/13/2019
Chronic lower back pain is a worldwide problem affecting millions of people living in various parts of the world. There are so many treatments available for chronic pain, but mostly put an adverse effect on your health. While replacing lower back pain with medications, these medicines produce other silent problems in our body that do not come into notice until upon laboratory test of some other disease opens up the real damage caused due to these medicines.

Chronic Lower Back Pain Treatment

1. Regular exercises are mostly recommended to treat this problem. It is a natural tool and does not depend upon any medications. You do exercises to make your bones and muscles stronger than ever. This, of course, makes your lower back pain more strong and you can easily bear sudden incidents that are quite famous for back pain issues.
2. The advent of computers in our world has changed the way we work in our office premises. We keep continuing working in front of a computer screen for 6-8 hours for the whole day. According to a survey, around 40% of back pain are the results of continuous sitting in front of a computer. As a treatment for this, you should buy a Molly Ortho cushion in your seats. It alleviates your lower back from most of the negative effects of continuous sitting.

3. You have seen children playing with a big ball. They sit on the ball and move from one location to another by jumping a little bit. This is an excellent exercise which makes your lower back muscle flexible and stronger. If you feel difficulty in moving, then you just sit on the ball and keep balanced yourself. This works great as well for the backside muscle.

4. Superman-style exercise is also good for chronic lower back pain. Under that, you lie on your stomach with the legs behind and hands fully stretched in front of you. Now, gradually raise your legs and arms from the floor with the power of your back muscles. Do not do it all in one day, try to lift your legs and hands around 3-4 inches daily if you are in severe lower back pain trouble.

5. A slipped disc is also the major reason for chronic lower back pain and it arises due to an imbalance in vertebrae. These vertebrae are segregated by fluid (which allows frictionless movement) and connective cartilage. When the connective cartilage pushes against the spinal nerves, it causes a lot of pain in the lower backside. A chiropractor must be contacted to treat for this misaligned spine problem. Otherwise, you will be making your case more worst.

6. Yoga is the center of focus for everyone. Yoga has been found to be very effective in treating various diseases. Chronic lower back pain is not an exception. People in Asian countries often ignore the importance of Yoga, believe me, it is worth to try. You have nothing to lose and you will get something which you will not find in any other treatment.

7. Physiotherapy is also regarded as the best chronic lower back pain treatment in this era. The importance of this treatment can be judged from this fact that around 3 out of 10 people are looking to make their career as a Physiotherapist. Usually, it uses a mix of heating pads, massager and ultraviolet rays to treat lower back pain.
